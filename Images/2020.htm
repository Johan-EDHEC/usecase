QS World University Rankings: Masters in Business Analytics Rankings 2020

Top 10 2020

Now featuring 83 institutions, this year’s ranking of the top business analytics postgraduate programs is our biggest yet. While MIT Sloan School of Management continues to top the ranking, there’s a new entrant at number two: UCLA’s Anderson School of Management.

Anderson actually out-performs MIT Sloan for value for money, one of the five indicators used to compile the ranking. For more information about these, consult our methodology.
